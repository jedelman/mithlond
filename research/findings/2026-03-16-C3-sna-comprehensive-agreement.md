### What Was Found
The **SNA Comprehensive Agreement** with Global Technical Systems (GTS), signed Oct 13, 2023, establishes **"Open Access"** in **Article VIII**. While GTS holds a 96-strand exclusivity for the first **18 months** (extendable to 60 months), **Section 8.4** reserves fiber for member Cities' **"intra- or inter-governmental uses."** **Section 8.6** mandates fair, reasonable, and non-discriminatory access for all "Approved ISPs."

### Source
[SNA Board Meeting Agenda Packet (2025-04-11)](https://www.hrpdcva.gov/uploads/docs/041125%20SNA%2006_Presentation%20GTS%20Comprehensive%20Agreement.pdf) and SNA Interim Agreement (2023).

### Verification Status
**VERIFIED** Article VIII structure and Section 8.4 "Governmental Use" exception via public board records.

### Implications for Mithlond
The **Section 8.4 "Governmental Use" exception** is the critical legal path for municipal compute. It explicitly allows the City of Norfolk to provision fiber for its own needs (e.g., connecting municipal nodes) without competing with GTS's commercial lease, provided it remains for intra-governmental use.

# Mithlond

**A research and advocacy project: community compute for Hampton Roads, Virginia.**

Mithlond applies the commons framework to data center infrastructure — bioregional sovereignty over compute, environmental accountability, and social equity for the AI era, focused on Norfolk and Hampton Roads.

Named for the Grey Havens: an Elvish port city, a place of intelligence, craft, and collective will organized around a particular place.

## What's Here

- `index.html` — Landing page: what this is, what we know, what we don't
- `blog/` — Research session log, published as we go
- `links.html` — Sources, allied organizations, essential journalism
- `css/style.css` — Site stylesheet

## Research Approach

We publish our work as we go — findings, sources, dead ends, and open questions. Research first, advocacy second. The process is part of the point.
